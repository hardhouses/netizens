  • Can I request specialized instruction or focus on specific areas of English learning?

    Yes, absolutely! We understand that every learner is unique and may have specific areas of focus or challenges in English learning. Our tutoring spaces are designed to cater to your individual needs.

  • Where can I receive classes?

    We offer flexible options for your convenience. You can choose to receive classes in a location that suits you best. We can meet you at your home, at our dedicated learning space, a local library, or online, depending on your preferences. We're committed to making your learning experience as comfortable and effective as possible.

  • What are your pricing and payment options?

    Our pricing is designed to be flexible and cater to your specific needs. If you're looking for personalized tutoring to help with your English classes, it's just $5 per hour for group sessions with a maximum of three people. Group sessions are open to students from the same school, year level, and studying the same texts. You and your friends can form groups and sign up together.

    If you're interested in our comprehensive English program, which includes our curriculum, engaging activities, as well as materials like photocopies and digital resources, the cost is $30 per person for group sessions with a maximum of three people. Individual enrollment is also available, and classes proceed without hesitation.

    We offer multiple payment options, including cash and money transfer to the following banks: BANPRO, LAFISE BANCENTRO, and FICOHSA. If you prefer classes at a library or your home, we kindly request payment in advance to ensure a seamless learning experience and avoid any last-minute cancellations or no-shows.

    Your convenience and satisfaction are our priorities, and we're here to provide a smooth and hassle-free payment process.
